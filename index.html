Internationalization Programmes

At Rank Global, our team works with top ranked universities in enabling and extending the reach of their internationalization programmes.
Our customized programmes help you build sustainable partnerships with institutions & potential student communities, across your desired geographies.
Many a time, we help universities and institutions to fine tune their global coverage. In-effect, Rank Global works as a ‘marketing arm’ of your institutions. 
Our work process involves direct engagement with students, senior administrators, decision makers & faculty.

University Relationships

Rank Global creates platforms for universities towards ‘an increased dialogue’ between senior decision makers, academics, researchers and students to understand each other’s strategic priorities.

We work with universities towards development and implementation of your strategies to partner with sister institutions across the ‘preferred regions’.

Specifically, we can help you create partnerships for:

  • Exchanges – Faculty, Student & Research
  • Research Partnerships
  • Articulation Programmes
  • Semester Abroad Programmes
  • Creating a Geographic Network for Research   Partnerships
  • Specific MOUs / Student Conclaves / Joint projects  with Industry and Government

On Campus Student & Faculty Interactions

We help build a long term relationship with universities through direct student interactions right ‘on-campus’, and along with their faculty. Over a period of time, this helps improve student quality in recruitment and faculty also gets integrated in the process.

Faculty Interaction: International Universities benefit the most when they interact with faculty of collaborating institutions on an ongoing basis. In addition, students as well as parents are most likely to consult the faculty when it comes to further studies. Today, with Top Autonomous/Private/Deemed Institutions evaluating Internationalization and Exchanges, a campus visit goes a long way in building reputations of the two institutions together.

Student Mobility

Rank Global works with universities to develop cross cultural trips to enable a student understanding of markets along with experiencing technical and business environments.
Our Mobility Programmes enable a familiarity platform to enhance student’s perspective of the Institution, the City & the Country.

Customized Study Tours

Inbound - India, China, Hong Kong, Australia

Inbound programmes are for International Universities who want to bring their students to India, China, Australia. Programmes can be customized for other geographies depending on your requirements. Usual duration of such programmes ranges from 10-14 days.

Key focus of these programmes are talks on the regions business environment, industry visits, interactions & workshops with local students and faculty. Usually these are in conjunction with a local institution or university who also provide academic support.

Outbound – Australia, Hong Kong, China, Turkey, Europe, UK

Outbound programmes are primarily focused on Indian Institutions, who wish to expose their students to international enviornments. Such programmes are in conjunction with leading universities abroad. Industry visits and special interest tours are integral to these programmes.

For schools, we take students to a University Tour for students who are interested in studying abroad. The one to two week programmes tour universities in a region, to help students enable understand the international academic environment. Talks with university officials and academics are integral to these tours.

Special Interest Study Tours

Special interest tours for school students are undertaken as an enabler to imbibe international best practices, understand cultures through exchanges and summer programmes in conjunction with international schools and universities.

  • Architecture Tours
  • History Appreciation Tours
  • Art and Culture Tours
  • Customized tours are developed based on specific brief
  • Faculty interest tours for faculties in particular or for their students

Research Internships

Students interested in Research Internships at universities, as a part of their programmes can apply to us. We create immersion programmes in conjunction with international universities, where interested students visit international campuses and meet professors. Usually these programmes are for a week and hosted by an international university.

About us

Headquartered at New Delhi, Rank Global Services engages with top educational institutions to promote their internationalization efforts. Our work area spans Indian sub-continent, China, Eastern Europe, Latin America, United States, South Asia & Australia.

Our objective is to establish our partner universities & Institutions ‘as brands’ within a geographic frame work by establishing their networks with the best institutions in the geographic region.

Our works spans both the spectrums – University ecosystems and Student Ecosystems.

Customized Solutions

All our proposals are customized to an individual requirement or objective, within a geographic framework. While we have a global network, we specifically forge relationships for you - rather than capitalizing on an existing network of relationships.




Our Process

While we engage with senior most administrators, faculty and directors at top most institutes / colleges and universities globally, our well researched process aims to develop customized programmes with partner universities to build an ongoing relationship for them.

We have in-depth understanding of the education environment in the geographies we work and our objective is to bring two quality institutions together towards a mutually rewarding academic understanding.

The process has been successful in India as well as other countries as it involves all major stake holders including administrators, faculty and students.... even alumni.
